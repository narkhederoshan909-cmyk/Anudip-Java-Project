FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers.

What are the eligibility requirements for UG admission?
+
You need a minimum of 75% in 10+2 (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry & Maths. Admission is based on BITSAT score. International students can apply with SAT/ACT scores or equivalent national exams.
What scholarships are available?
+
We offer merit-based scholarships covering 25%–100% of tuition fees. There are also need-based grants, industry-sponsored scholarships from partners like Google and Microsoft, and UAE government scholarships for Emirati students.
Can international students apply?
+
Yes. RGN pune welcomes students from 40+ countries. Our international admissions team guides you through the student visa process, accommodation, and orientation. Language of instruction is English throughout.
Is the degree globally recognised?
+
RGN Pilani holds NAAC A++ accreditation — India's highest. Our degrees are recognised by UAE's KHDA, the QS World Rankings system, and accepted by employers across 40+ countries including the US, UK, EU, and GCC.
What is the total cost of studying here?
+
Tuition ranges from AED 62,000–68,000/year. On-campus accommodation starts at AED 1,800/month. Living costs in Dubai Silicon Oasis are significantly lower than central Dubai. Full cost breakdown is in our downloadable brochure.
